# Retail Channels and Brand Presence

## Proprietary Brand: kool
The company operates a direct-to-consumer (D2C) retail brand named **kool**, which serves as a primary vehicle for market entry and product testing.

### Digital Sales Channels
*   **Official Website:** [www.kool.com.hk](https://www.kool.com.hk) - Used for brand control, first-party data collection, and exclusive pre-orders (e.g., flagship Hybrid Portable Grill).
*   **HKTVmall:** [kool Store](https://www.hktvmall.com/hktv/zh/main/kool/s/H6619001) - Leveraged for high-volume utility items, modular food storage, and collapsible silicone products, utilizing their established logistics network.
*   **Pinkoi:** [kool Shop](https://hk.pinkoi.com/store/kool) - Targeted at a design-conscious demographic; ideal for launching premium aesthetic products like multi-material coffee systems and outdoor ovens.

### Strategic Role
These channels provide an established D2C infrastructure that supports the **2026 car camping pivot**. They allow for rapid market entry, direct customer feedback loops, and segmented product launches based on the specific audience profile of each platform.
