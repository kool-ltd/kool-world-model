# Company Overview

## General Information
- **Industry:** Manufacturing
- **Specialization:** Kitchenware and Outdoor Lifestyle Gear

## Business Focus
Specialized manufacturing entity. While rooted in the kitchenware sector, the company is currently executing a strategic pivot into the car camping market for 2026. This shift aims to leverage existing manufacturing infrastructure to enter a high-margin, lifestyle-driven outdoor segment.

## Manufacturing Capabilities
- **Plastic Injection Molding:** Strong internal capability for rigid components and impact-resistant shells.
- **Silicone Molding:** Specialized expertise in Liquid Silicone Molding (LSR). This allows for the production of food-safe, high-temperature resistant, and highly durable components.
- **Sheet Metal Fabrication (Partnership):** As of April 2026, the company has established a strategic partnership with a specialized metal factory capable of producing sheet metal components for grills and outdoor ovens.

## 2026 Strategic Roadmap
- **Market Pivot:** Entry into the car camping niche.
- **Research Priorities:** Market trend analysis (smart technology and sustainable materials), supply chain operational audits, and international growth strategies.
- **Product Development Philosophy:** Focus on "Hybrid Design"—integrating metal, plastic, and LSR to create differentiated, durable, and space-saving solutions that competitors cannot easily replicate.
