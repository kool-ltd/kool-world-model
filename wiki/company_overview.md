# Company Overview

## General Information
- **Industry:** Manufacturing
- **Specialization:** Kitchenware and Outdoor Lifestyle Gear

## Business Structure
The company operates two distinct business streams:

### 1. B2B / ODM (The 2026 Car Camping Pivot)
- **Target:** Business customers and outdoor brands.
- **Focus:** Leveraging manufacturing infrastructure (Plastic, LSR, and Sheet Metal) to enter the high-margin car camping market.
- **Value Proposition:** "Hybrid Design" manufacturing for durable, space-saving outdoor gear.

### 2. B2C / Retail Brand (kool)
- **Target:** Retail consumers, primarily in Hong Kong.
- **Focus:** Premium kitchenware, food storage, and coffee systems.
- **Value Proposition:** Utility, aesthetics, and high-quality materials.

## Manufacturing Capabilities
- **Plastic Injection Molding:** Rigid components and impact-resistant shells.
- **Silicone Molding:** Specialized Liquid Silicone Molding (LSR) for food-safe, high-temperature resistant components.
- **Sheet Metal Fabrication (Partnership):** Strategic partnership for producing grills and outdoor ovens.
