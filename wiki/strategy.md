# Strategy and Product Development

## Car Camping Pivot (2026)
As of April 2026, the company is shifting focus toward the car camping market. The design team has identified three primary strategic directions for product development:

### 1. Coffee Systems
- **Strategic Value:** High-margin entry point for building brand loyalty.
- **Design Goals:** Focus on modularity (e.g., nesting pour-over or French press systems), heat retention, premium aesthetics, and extreme durability (impact resistance).

### 2. Modular Food Storage
- **Strategic Value:** High-volume utility products addressing gaps in trunk organization and space management.
- **Design Goals:** Stackable, collapsible, and multi-functional designs. Key innovations include airtight containers that double as preparation surfaces or integrate with standard cooler dimensions.

### 3. Grilling & Cooking
- **Strategic Value:** Core strength and "flagship hero" product category. Closest alignment with legacy kitchenware expertise.
- **Design Goals:** Wind-resistant, easy-to-clean, and space-saving features. Priorities include flat-pack grills and nesting cookware sets with high-durability, non-stick coatings.
