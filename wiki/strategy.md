# Strategy and Product Development

## Car Camping Pivot (2026)
As of April 2026, the company is shifting focus toward the car camping market. The strategy emphasizes utilizing in-house plastic injection and Liquid Silicone Molding (LSR) to minimize outsourcing and maximize margin.

### 1. Modular Food Storage (Primary Priority)
- **Strategic Value:** High-volume utility products addressing gaps in trunk organization and space management. This is the "clear winner" for current factory capabilities.
- **Design Goals:** Stackable, collapsible, and multi-functional designs. 
- **Technical Application:** Use plastic injection for rigid frames/lids and LSR for collapsible, airtight, and temperature-resistant bodies.

### 2. Coffee Systems (Secondary Priority)
- **Strategic Value:** High-margin entry point for building brand loyalty through premium, unbreakable brewing solutions.
- **Design Goals:** Focus on modularity (e.g., nesting systems), heat retention, and extreme durability.
- **Technical Application:** LSR for collapsible pour-over cones, seals, and grips; plastic injection for impact-resistant outer shells.

### 3. Grilling & Cooking (Support/Prep-Ware Focus)
- **Strategic Value:** Core strength and "flagship hero" product category. 
- **Design Goals:** Wind-resistant and space-saving features.
- **Technical Application:** Shift from heavy metal fabrication toward high-heat LSR cooking accessories (spatulas, tongs, nesting prep bowls) and plastic-injected "kitchen-in-a-box" carrying solutions.

## Strategic Recommendation
Prioritize **Modular Food Storage** and **Coffee Systems** to utilize 100% of in-house plastic and LSR capabilities before expanding into heavy metal fabrication.
