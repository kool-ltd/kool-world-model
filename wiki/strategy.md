# Strategy and Product Development

## B2B ODM Strategy: Car Camping Pivot (2026)
The company is targeting business customers in the car camping market by offering integrated manufacturing solutions. The strategy emphasizes a hybrid material approach (Metal, LSR, Plastic) for grills, modular storage, and coffee systems.

## B2C Retail Strategy: kool Brand
Focuses on the retail kitchenware market, utilizing D2C infrastructure to sell food storage, preparation tools, and coffee systems.

### Strategic Imperatives based on Sales Data
1.  **Capitalize on High-Volume Winners:** Maintain inventory and visibility for the `#1 Silicone` series (especially Red variants) and `Knife Sets`, which are established revenue drivers.
2.  **Bundle Stagnant Inventory:** High-value sets (e.g., Ultimate Set) and innovative products (e.g., OneLid, OneScrub) currently showing zero sales volume should be cross-sold via bundles or targeted campaigns on the official website to leverage traffic from top sellers.
